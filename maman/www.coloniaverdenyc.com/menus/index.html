Domaine Du Pech

About two hours East from Bordeaux, we find Domaine Du Pech, run by a husband and wife. This is outside of Bordeaux but with all Bordeaux tradition, the way it was always intended. The luxury here is the ability to give wines time to mature and come into their own. 200-year-old barrels, 2004 current releases, and a Sauvignon Blanc with age. These wines have been waiting patiently to be drank and will now disappear so grab a bottle while they last!
